Review the current plan with a designer's eye.

Requirements:

- determine whether the plan has UI or UX scope before continuing
- rate design completeness by review pass
- add missing design decisions directly into the plan when they are obvious
- ask only one real design choice question at a time
- end with completion scores, unresolved decisions, and next design steps
